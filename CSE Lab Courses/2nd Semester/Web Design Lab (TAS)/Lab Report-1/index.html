Historical Places to Visit




Patisar Rabindra Kachari Bari
is one of the preserved antiquities of Bangladesh located in Atrai upazila of Naogaon district and the memorial place of world poet Rabindranath Tagore. It is located on the bank of the river Nagar in the village of Patisar of the upazila.
Learn More.



Kusumba Mosque
Located in Manda Upazila of Naogaon District, Bangladesh, it is in the village of Kusumba, its namesake. Built in 1558-59, it is a national heritage site. Often called "The Black Gem of Bangladesh," this historic mosque stands as a symbol of architectural elegance.

Varendra Research Museum
It is the country's oldest museum, showcasing a rich collection of ancient artifacts, sculptures, and inscriptions from Bengal's history.
